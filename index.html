📚 Drill Library
Spot Shooting
Beginner 🔁 5×10
▾
Place markers at 5 spots around the arc. Make 10 shots from each spot before moving. Focus on consistent form — feet, aim, follow-through.
Around the World
Beginner 🔁 1 round
▾
7 spots from corner to corner. Make a shot before advancing. Miss = start that spot again. Complete the full arc for 1 round.
Catch & Shoot
Intermediate 🔁 3×20
▾
Set the ball on the floor, step to it, catch with hands ready, and shoot in one fluid motion. Simulate catching a pass from a teammate.
Off-Dribble Pull-Up
Intermediate 🔁 3×10
▾
Dribble hard left or right, plant and pull up for a jumper. Focus on balance at the moment of elevation — don't fade sideways.
Elbow Series
Advanced 🔁 4×15
▾
Alternate between left and right elbow. Each make = step back 1 foot. Miss = reset to elbow. Works mid-range consistency under fatigue.
Free Throw Pressure
Beginner 🔁 10 sets
▾
Shoot 2 free throws per set. If you miss either = 10 pushups. Builds real game pressure on free throws. Track made sets.
Two-Ball Dribble
Beginner 🔁 3×60s
▾
Dribble both balls simultaneously at hip height. Keep eyes up. Progress to alternating rhythm once both balls stay controlled.
Crossover Series
Intermediate 🔁 3×30s
▾
Low, hard crossovers — contact point at knee height. Crossover → between legs → behind back. Keep the ball below the waist always.
Figure 8
Beginner 🔁 3×45s
▾
Dribble the ball in a figure 8 around both legs continuously. Stay low, keep head up. Increase speed each set.
1-Dribble Pull-Up
Advanced 🔁 3×10 each side
▾
From wing, one hard dribble attack, gather, and pull up. React to imaginary defender. Work both left and right attacks equally.
Jab Step Series
Beginner 🔁 3×10 each
▾
From triple threat: jab right, jab left, jab-cross, jab-go. Sell each jab with your eyes and shoulders before reacting.
Pivot & Seal
Intermediate 🔁 3×10
▾
Catch ball, front pivot, reverse pivot, drop step. Build pivot speed until the footwork is automatic — no hesitation.
Hop Step Finish
Advanced 🔁 3×10
▾
Attack baseline, gather off two feet (hop step), finish at the backboard. Alternate sides. Key: keep the ball high through the gather.
